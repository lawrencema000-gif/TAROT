## Email Design Notes

- **From name:** Arcana
- **From email:** hello@tarotlife.app (or noreply@)
- **Unsubscribe:** Required — bottom of every email
- **Style:** Dark background (#0a0a0f), gold accents (#d4a853), minimal layout
- **CTA buttons:** Gold gradient, single primary CTA per email
- **Tone:** Mystical but warm. Never pushy. Never salesy. The app sells itself.
- **Personalization:** Use first name, zodiac sign, streak count where available
