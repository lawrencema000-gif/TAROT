## Full description (max 4000 chars)

**Arcana is a calm, beautiful daily practice for people who want to understand themselves a little better.**

One ritual a day. That's it. Pull a tarot card, read your horoscope, write in your journal — take what resonates, leave what doesn't. No noise, no doom-scrolling, no pushy notifications.

**What's inside**

✦ **Daily tarot** — full 78-card deck with modern, clear-eyed interpretations. Keywords, love and career meanings, reflection prompts, upright and reversed.

✦ **Personalised horoscope** — daily, weekly, and monthly forecasts based on your sun, moon, and rising signs. Themes for love, career, mood, and a lucky color to carry you through the day.

✦ **Birth chart** — complete natal chart with planet-in-sign and planet-in-house interpretations, natal aspects, and transit forecasts. Learn the astrology language of your own chart.

✦ **Journal** — a small private space with guided prompts tied to each day's card. Write for two minutes and notice the patterns that appear over weeks.

✦ **Personality quizzes** — MBTI, Enneagram, Big Five, Love Language, and Attachment Style. Take them once, get interpretations that weave into your tarot and horoscope context.

✦ **Compatibility** — compare your chart with someone else's and see where you flow and where you friction.

✦ **Streaks & trophies** — gentle consistency nudges. Not gamified, just acknowledged.

**Made for thoughtful people**

Arcana doesn't predict, promise, or panic. It's a mirror. The tone is warm and grounded; the design is dark, calm, and focused. Every reading can be saved, shared, or simply read and released.

**Privacy-first**

Your journal is private. Your chart data never leaves our servers except to render readings for you. We don't sell anything to anyone.

**Free, with optional premium**

Most features are free forever. Premium adds unlimited AI-generated readings for custom questions, full natal chart interpretations, and an ad-free experience.

**Available in English, Japanese, Korean, and Chinese.**

Whether you're new to tarot or have read cards for years, Arcana is built to be your quiet companion. One ritual a day.
