What makes Arcana different

  • Real ephemeris, not generic horoscopes. Arcana calculates actual planetary positions from your birth moment using astronomical data, rather than serving the same sun-sign content to everyone born in the same month.
  • Human Design computed correctly. Unlike most lightweight apps that fake Human Design output, Arcana computes the 88°-solar-arc Design moment, maps all 13 activations to the 64 I-Ching gates, and derives Type, Authority, and Profile from actual channel definitions.
  • Jungian, not predictive. Every reading frames the question as a mirror rather than a fortune. The AI dream interpreter uses Carl Jung's archetypal framework; the daily horoscope ends with a reflection prompt, not a prediction.
  • 13 divination systems in one app. Tarot, Western astrology, Bazi (Chinese 四柱), I-Ching, Human Design, Elder Futhark runes, dice oracle, Feng Shui Bagua, dream work, mood tracking, partner synastry, and more.
  • Built for four languages natively. Not machine-translated — hand-localized copy for Japanese, Korean, and Simplified Chinese markets from day one.

Quotable founder statement

"The wellness apps I used never felt honest with me. They'd tell me my future in generic terms, or turn the ancient systems into games. I wanted to build something that treated symbols with respect — that used astronomy to actually compute your chart, that let AI read your dream through Jung rather than pretend to predict your week. Arcana is what I wished had existed."
