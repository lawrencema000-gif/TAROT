Card Description

Three women raise their cups high in exuberant celebration, dancing together in a garden abundant with harvest fruits and flowers. They form a circle of mutual support and joy, each one contributing to the collective happiness. Their bodies move with spontaneous joy, their cups overflow, and the bounty at their feet speaks to the abundance that friendship and community create. This is the card of chosen family, of finding your people and celebrating together.

↑
Upright Meaning

Celebration, friendship, community, creative collaboration, and joyous gatherings. The Three of Cups is the card of sisterhood, friendship, and the happiness that comes from sharing life's beautiful moments with those who truly see and celebrate you.

↓
Reversed Meaning

Overindulgence, gossip, isolation, or feeling left out. The celebration may become excessive, friendships may be superficial or toxic, or you may be withdrawing from community when you need it most.

♡ Love & Relationships

Celebrating relationships with friends and community support for your romantic life. This card can indicate celebrations like engagements, weddings, or anniversaries where community gathers to witness your love. It also speaks to the importance of maintaining friendships alongside romantic partnerships - your partner should integrate well with your chosen family. Sometimes this card suggests a love triangle or romantic choice between people.

◈ Career & Finances

Team success, collaborative projects, and workplace celebrations. Creative partnerships flourish and group efforts yield abundant results. This card favors networking, team-building, and careers involving events, hospitality, or bringing people together. Your colleagues are celebrating shared wins and the work environment is harmonious and supportive.

◉ Yes or No Reading
Yes

This card's energy of Celebration and Friendship leans toward a positive outcome.

✎ Reflection Prompt
Who are my people - my chosen family? When was the last time I celebrated with those I love? How can I create more joyful community in my life?
