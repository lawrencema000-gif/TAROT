## 0. Executive summary

**Goal (90 days):** 25,000 new accounts · 2,500 paid trials · 750 paid conversions (30% trial→paid) · $4–6k MRR ramp.

**Core thesis:** Tarot apps win on **trust, daily ritual, and personalized depth**. Arcana already has the product depth (78 cards, 40 spreads, /astrology + /numerology + /crystals + /glossary hubs, Mirror stats, Fool's Journey progression). What's missing is **distribution**. This 90-day plan focuses 80% on distribution, 20% on conversion polish.

**Channel mix priority (ranked by tarot-app conversion data + your existing assets):**

1. **TikTok organic** — 35% of effort (highest discovery for spiritual content)
2. **Instagram organic** — 25% of effort (best for retention + brand)
3. **SEO + content** — 15% of effort (foundation already shipped — amplify)
4. **Pinterest organic** — 10% of effort (long-tail traffic, often forgotten)
5. **Paid (Google + Meta + TikTok)** — 10% of effort (start small, scale winners)
6. **Email + lifecycle** — 5% of effort (3-part course is shipped, build broadcast cadence)

**90-day budget recommendation:** $3,000–6,000 total
- $1,500 paid ads (split below)
- $1,000 creator/UGC ($50–100 per micro-creator × 10–20)
- $300 tools (Later/Buffer, ManyChat, Notion)
- $200 stock / Canva Pro / asset creation
- Reserve: $500–3,000 for scaling winning ads

## 1. Ideal customer profiles (ICPs)

Three personas you market to. Each gets distinct creative + targeting.

### Persona A — "The Daily Ritualist" (40% of target)
- **Who:** Women, 22–35, urban, knowledge worker / creative
- **Behavior:** Already does morning journaling, wellness apps (Calm, Co-Star), yoga
- **Pain:** Wants depth beyond Co-Star's 2-line predictions; wants a daily practice that compounds
- **Hook:** "Your daily 5-minute ritual"
- **Channel:** Instagram, TikTok (#witchtok with #softlife crossover)
- **Conversion path:** TikTok video → /tarot-meanings/the-fool → 3-part email course → trial → paid yearly

### Persona B — "The Curious Beginner" (35% of target)
- **Who:** Women + men, 18–28, students or early career, exploring spirituality
- **Behavior:** Just bought their first deck, watches Lana Del Rey on TikTok, reads Co-Star
- **Pain:** Doesn't trust their interpretation; wants a teacher
- **Hook:** "Learn tarot in 3 emails" (we already have this — push it harder)
- **Channel:** TikTok (#learntarot), Pinterest, YouTube Shorts
- **Conversion path:** TikTok → /signup with email-course magnet → 3 emails → free app account → trial after 7 days

### Persona C — "The Returning Reader" (25% of target)
- **Who:** Women 30–55, has read tarot for years, wants better tools
- **Behavior:** Owns 3+ decks, follows tarot creators on IG/Substack, paid subscriber to The Tarot Lady or Biddy Tarot
- **Pain:** Existing apps are too shallow (Co-Star) or too clunky (Labyrinthos has weak UX); wants depth + design
- **Hook:** "The reader's app — Mirror your patterns, journal every spread, design your own layouts"
- **Channel:** Instagram (#tarotreader, #professionaltarot), Substack collabs, Pinterest
- **Conversion path:** IG carousel / Substack mention → /spreads/celtic-cross deep page → /spreads/builder demo → yearly subscription

## 2. Positioning + brand voice

### Positioning statement (one-liner)
> **"Arcana is the daily tarot app for people who want depth, not vibes."**

### Three messaging pillars
1. **Depth** — 78 cards × astrology + numerology + combinations. Mirror stats over time. /spreads/builder for custom layouts.
2. **Ritual** — One pull a day. Five minutes. The streak compounds. (Tagline: "Know yourself. One ritual a day.")
3. **No bullshit** — Ad-free Premium. No "manifest your dream life" fluff. Real Rider-Waite-Smith tradition + modern psychology.

### Voice rules
- **Use:** "ritual," "practice," "the Fool's Journey," "the deck knows," "your inner reader"
- **Avoid:** "manifest!", "high vibes", "✨🔮", "babe / queen / babes", overly mystical filler
- **Sentence rhythm:** Short. Then longer with one more idea. Rare three-clause sentences.

## 3. Channel strategy — what to do where

### TikTok (35% of effort)

**Cadence:** 1 post/day, 7 days/week. Yes, every day. Tarot performs best with ritual content frequency.

**Best post times (AEST → audience time-shifted to US for max reach):**
- Mon/Wed/Fri: 10am AEST = 7pm prev-day US East / 4pm prev-day US West
- Tue/Thu/Sat: 7pm AEST (catch SE Asia + AU + EU morning)
- Sun: 11am AEST (slow day, lighter content)

**Post types (rotate weekly):**

| Format | Frequency | Hook example | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Card of the day** | 3×/week | "Today's pull: [card]. If you saw this, you needed to hear—" | "App link in bio for your free pull" |
| **Spread tutorial** | 2×/week | "I bet you've never tried this 5-card spread for [topic]" | "Full spread guide: link in bio" |
| **Astrology + tarot crossover** | 1×/week | "Mercury retrograde + The Tower in your reading? Here's what it means" | "Get your transit reading: link" |
| **POV / storytime** | 1×/week | "POV: you pulled the Death card and panicked. Here's what it actually means" | "Free 3-part tarot course: link" |

**Critical rules:**
- First 3 seconds = THE hook. No "hi guys" intros.
- Captions ≤ 100 chars. Hashtag dump in first comment, not caption.
- Hashtags: 8–12 mix of broad (`#tarot #witchtok #zodiac`) + niche (`#celticcross #tarotbeginners #mercuryinretrograde`) + branded (`#arcanatarot #tarotlife`)
- Hook formats that consistently work in this niche: "POV", "Tell me you're a [sign] without telling me", "Reading I never thought I'd give", "This card stopped me"
- Use TikTok's SOUND library (trending audio) — check daily, jump on trends within 48 hours
- **Reply with video** to top comments — 2–3× engagement vs text replies

**Creator-style baseline:** look at @theshapeshifter.tarot, @tarot.witch, @taromancer for top-performing formats. Don't copy — match the format-to-platform fit.

**Campaigns to run:**

   - Negative keywords: "free", "online" (too top-funnel)
   - Expected CPA: $8–15

3. **Performance Max — app installs** ($50/month, run only on Android until iOS ships)
   - Goal: app installs at < $1 each in tier-2 markets
   - Tier-2 markets work because tarot apps have global appeal — India, Brazil, Mexico, Indonesia have lower CPI ($0.20–$0.80) and high engagement

#### Meta Ads ($500 over 90 days)

**Campaign 1: Lead-magnet email course** ($250 / 90 days)
- Objective: Conversion → Email signup
- Creative: 4 variants of UGC-style video showing "I learned tarot in 3 emails"
- Targeting: Women 22–45, interests: "Astrology", "Tarot", "Spirituality", "Co-Star"
- Lookalikes after 100 conversions: 1% LAL of email signups
- Expected: 200–400 emails @ $0.60–1.50 CAC

**Campaign 2: App install — yearly trial** ($250 / 90 days)
- Objective: App promotion (Android first, iOS when live)
- Creative: One static + one carousel + one Reel-style video

- Targeting: Custom audience of email subscribers (warm) + 1% LAL
- Expected CPI: $1–3, install→trial rate 30%+, trial→paid 20–30%

#### TikTok Ads ($300 over 90 days)

**Boost only proven organic videos.** Don't run cold creative.

Process:
- Wait until an organic post hits 50k+ views
- Boost it for $50/3 days targeting same demo
- If CPI stays under $2, scale to $100/week
- Cap at $300 total until you have data

#### Common ad-creative principles

- **Hook in first 1 second.** "Wait don't scroll — this card means [X]" beats brand intro.
- **No corporate B-roll.** UGC-style outperforms agency creative 3:1 in this niche.
- **Show the app, but only briefly.** 2-3 second product shot embedded in lifestyle content.
- **Always have a Reply-To-Story / IG-DM CTA option for warm audience** — tarot has high reply rate, and replies → app installs convert ~5×.

## 10. Risks + experiments

### Top 3 risks

1. **TikTok algorithm change** kills your reach mid-campaign.
   **Mitigation:** Cross-post everything to IG Reels + YouTube Shorts so you're not platform-dependent.

2. **App Store / Play Store rejection or de-listing.**
   **Mitigation:** Web (tarotlife.app) is your primary funnel anyway. Don't over-index on one storefront.

3. **A creator partnership goes wrong** (negative review, controversy).
   **Mitigation:** Avoid mid+ tier creators with controversy history. Stick to 80%+ nano/micro who are aligned with the values.

### 5 experiments to run during 90 days

| Week | Experiment | Hypothesis | Success criteria |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2 | Free 3-card daily reading on home page (no signup) | Reduces signup friction, captures more emails further down funnel | Email captures up 50% |
| 4 | A/B test landing page tagline ("Know yourself" vs "The reader's tarot app") | Persona C messaging converts better | Conversion rate +10% on Pinterest traffic |
| 6 | TikTok ad with creator UGC vs studio creative | UGC outperforms 2:1 | UGC CPI < 60% of studio CPI |
| 8 | Weekly Premium-only Mirror recap email | Reduces month-2 churn | Retention +5pp at day 60 |
| 10 | "Send a free reading to a friend" referral feature | Viral coefficient ≥ 0.3 | 200+ shared readings/week |
