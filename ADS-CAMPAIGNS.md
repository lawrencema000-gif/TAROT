## CAMPAIGN 1: Google App Campaign

### Setup (play.google.com/console → Advertising)

Google App Campaigns are the simplest — you give Google text, images, and a budget, and it automatically creates ads across Search, YouTube, Google Display Network, and Play Store.

**Campaign Settings:**
- Campaign type: **App promotion**
- Goal: **Install volume**
- App: com.arcana.app
- Daily budget: **$5–10**
- Target CPI bid: **$0.50–1.50** (start at $1, adjust after data)
- Locations: **United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia**
- Languages: **English**

### Ad Text (Google uses these to auto-generate ads)

**Headlines (max 30 chars each — provide 5):**
```
Daily Tarot & Horoscope
Free Tarot Card Readings
Know Yourself Daily
Your Daily Cosmic Ritual
78 Cards, 6 Spreads, Free
```

**Descriptions (max 90 chars each — provide 5):**
```
Pull tarot cards, read your horoscope, and journal — all free. Start your ritual today.
Free tarot readings with 78 cards and 6 spreads. Daily horoscope personalized to your sign.
MBTI, Enneagram, Love Language — 5 free personality quizzes in one app. Discover yourself.
Your daily ritual: horoscope, tarot, journal. Build streaks and level up. Free to start.
Personalized birth chart, daily horoscope, tarot readings. No paywall. Download free.
```

### Images to Upload
Upload 3–5 images (1200x628 or 1200x1200):
- Screenshot of the app's tarot reading screen
- Screenshot of the daily horoscope
- The card fan from your landing page hero
- App icon on dark background with tagline

### YouTube Video (optional but powerful)
Record a 15–30 second screen recording:
- Open app → pull a card → show the result → "Free on Google Play"

## TRACKING & OPTIMIZATION

### Week 1: Collect Data
- Run all campaigns at minimum budget
- Don't change anything for 3–5 days (let algorithms learn)
- Track: Cost per install (CPI), click-through rate (CTR), install rate

### Week 2: Optimize
| Metric | Good | Action if Bad |
|--------|------|---------------|
| CPI (Google) | < $1.50 | Lower bid, tighten geo targeting |
| CPI (Meta) | < $2.00 | Test new creatives, narrow audience |
| CTR | > 1% | Good — scale budget |
| CTR | < 0.5% | Change creative — image/copy isn't working |
| Install rate | > 30% | Play Store listing is good |
| Install rate | < 15% | Improve Play Store screenshots/description |

### Week 3+: Scale Winners
- Kill ads with CPI > $3
- Double budget on ads with CPI < $1
- Create new variations of winning ads
- Test zodiac-specific ads (all 12 signs)
